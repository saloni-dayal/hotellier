Kaldan Samudra Resort

Kaldan means an immortal place in Paradise, and we as Brand Ambassadors, we create lifestyle and luxury experiences for our discerning Guests which will remain immortal in their memories. Kaldan is located on the beautiful shores of Bay of Bengal, 2 Kms before the Temple city of Mahabalipuram.
